1.Internet Protocol (IP) address


2.Device type and operating system


3.Links and advertisements clicked


4.Time spent interacting with the Services


5.Other usage statistics.


We use this Log Data to:


1.Administer the Services.


2.Analyze usage patterns (either ourselves or through third parties) to improve and enhance the Services. This includes adding features, increasing functionality, and tailoring the Services to better meet user needs and preferences.


3.Generate aggregate, non-identifying insights into Service usage, utilizing the IP address for this purpose.

Mobile Device Information

 

When you access our Services via a mobile device, we collect device identifiers such as serial number, MAC address, or UDID. We use this data to improve and personalize your experience.

 

Advertising & Third Party Products and Services

 

The Services rely on advertising for support. To enable this and improve ad relevance, we collect limited data, including usage patterns (frequency, duration) and your mobile device's encrypted unique ID. This anonymized ID is shared with analytics companies, advertisers, and advertising networks so they can measure ad performance and deliver relevant ads. To safeguard your identity, encryption ensures these partners cannot personally identify you. They might also utilize other non-personal information (like other apps installed on your device, mobile websites visited, and general location such as zip code) to analyze and target ads across different platforms. Additionally, we may share encrypted data you've provided, enabling partners to enrich it with their own non-personal data solely for advertising analysis and targeting.
